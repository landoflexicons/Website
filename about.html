Vision


English, as a universal language, plays an inevitable role in the lives of all students. We aim to endow LSRW talents and enrich lexical abilities in students, bolstering them for various examinations such as IELTS, TOEFL, and GRE implicitly, thus driving away students fear of English and assisting them to emerge with flying colors.

Mission

Our mission is to ameliorate the vocabulary and communication skills of the students through memes and fun events.We also strive to augment the confidence level and professional speaking capabilities among the students by making them to get exposed through various domains of the accent in a collaborative manner and in discrete way.

Why LOL

  • 1) We develop vocabulary skills through fun and engaging way
  • 2) We have memes for better remembrance of the vocabulary
  • 3) We develop the literary skills
  • 4) We help you to understand on how to use the words in a proper form
  • 5) We help students to prepare for IELTS and TOEFL
Land of Lexicons is for thinkers, readers, speakers, creators, listeners and writers. It is the place where students have freedom to think, read, speak, create and write on the topics of their own interest and choice by using newly learnt words. Normal things are just ordinary but special things are extraordinary, likewise Land of Lexicons also has its own speciality where it is a platform to explore and experience the world of vocabulary through memes and all other activities of the club in a fun filled learning.
